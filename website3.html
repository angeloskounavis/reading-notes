Best Traveling Destinations

Greece

Greece is one of the most beautiful destinations one can visit. Some of the reasons for that is due to the warm and crystal clear waters of the mediterranean sea. Along the coast of Greece you will find plenty of beach bars, cafe shops and restaurants (all for cheap!!).

If you are a fun of history then you will have 2,000 years of ancient Greek history to explore. And if by any chance you are a foodie. Well, let's not forget about the Greek salad, Gyro and Mousaka(among other delicious dishes).

Italy

Italy is one of the most popular vacation destinations in the world. Some of the most popular destinations in Italy are Rome, Venice, Milan and Florence. Each of these cities has their own history and places to discover.

If you find yourself visiting any of these cities don't forget to try the local restaurants, you will not regret it. The different variations of pastas, pizzas and desserts like tiramisu will please even the toughest critics out there.
